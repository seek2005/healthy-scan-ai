About Me

Josue Daniel Torres

My name is Josue Daniel Torres. I am a husband, father, U.S. Army Reserve weekend warrior, and student pursuing an Associate of Science in Computer Technology.

Healthy Scan AI started as a personal solution. During a pediatric visit, my youngest daughter Camila’s doctor mentioned that she was slightly overweight for her age and encouraged us to pay closer attention to the nutrition labels of the foods we buy. Like many busy parents, I don’t always have time to read every label in detail.

"This capstone project uses my technical skills to tackle a real-life challenge: quickly understanding whether a product supports my family’s health and what healthier alternatives exist."
