About Us.

Our Mission

HealthyScan AI was built with one mission in mind: Help parents easily understand nutrition labels in real-world settings. As a parent, a tech student, and a member of the U.S. Army Reserve, I understand the difficulty of pausing mid-shopping to analyze every label in detail.

This was a passion project that began after a talk with my daughter's pediatrician, where I quickly realized most labels were made with the world's most pressing problems in mind not with parents in mind who are trying to shop quickly while also worrying about a thousand other things.

Our tool aims to use AI to transform a difficult-to-read Nutrition Facts label into a simple, actionable summary. Instead of scrolling through and interpreting a long list of numbers, HealthyScan AI surfaces important information about the levels of three key nutrients: sugar, sodium, and saturated fat, and visualizes how they may impact key demographic groups: children, adults, and older adults.

"Finally, it also provides a recommended smarter alternative within the same product category so you don't just know what is wrong with a product but have a specific recommendation for a better option for your family."

HealthyScan AI is a student built prototype intended for research and demonstration purposes only. It is not a medical device nor a substitute for professional dietary consultation. It is intended to aid shoppers in making more informed and health-conscious decisions while not overwhelming them and to help busy parents make healthy choices that feel quicker and more convenient. However, all final decisions regarding health and diet should always be made in conjunction with a qualified professional.

Prototype Capabilities.

Image Upload & Inference

Simulates scanning by analyzing uploaded label images. The system automatically infers categories (e.g., soda, cereal) from visual data.

Health Score Engine

A proprietary logic that penalizes negative nutrients (sugars, sodium) while rewarding positive ones to generate an unbiased score.

Age-Specific Analysis

Generates breakdown tables specifically tailored for three distinct groups: Children (4-8), Adults (19-50), and Seniors (51+).

Smart Alternatives

Automatically identifies and suggests the healthiest product available within the same category to offer an immediate actionable swap.
