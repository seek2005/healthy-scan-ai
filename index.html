About Me

Josue Daniel Torres

My name is Josue Daniel Torres. I am a husband, father, U.S. Army Reserve weekend warrior, and student pursuing an Associate of Science in Computer Technology.

Healthy Scan AI started as a personal solution. During a pediatric visit, my youngest daughter Camila’s doctor mentioned that she was slightly overweight for her age and encouraged us to pay closer attention to the nutrition labels of the foods we buy. Like many busy parents, I don’t always have time to read every label in detail.

"This capstone project uses my technical skills to tackle a real-life challenge: quickly understanding whether a product supports my family’s health and what healthier alternatives exist in the same category."

Prototype Capabilities

Image Upload & Inference

Simulates scanning by analyzing uploaded label images. The system automatically infers categories (e.g., soda, cereal, yogurt) from visual data without manual input.

Health Score Engine

A proprietary logic that penalizes negative nutrients (added sugars, sodium, saturated fats) while rewarding positive ones (fiber, protein) to generate an unbiased score.

Age-Specific Analysis

Generates breakdown tables specifically tailored for three distinct groups: Children (4-8), Adults (19-50), and Seniors (51+).

Smart Alternatives

Automatically identifies and suggests the healthiest product available within the same category to offer an immediate actionable swap.

User Guide

1

Snap or Upload

Take a clear photo of the product’s Nutrition Facts label or barcode with your phone.

2

Scan & Analyze

Go to the Scanner tab, upload your image, and click the big "Scan & Analyze" button.

3

Review Insights

Read the AI summary, check the table for your age group, and click the alternative to buy a healthier option.
